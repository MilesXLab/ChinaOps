**Last Updated:** Jan 2026 | **Author:** TechDadShanghai

2.  **The Metro Failover:** In Shanghai, the Metro is faster than taxis during 8-10 AM and 5-7 PM. If a Didi says "30 mins wait," head to the nearest station. Use Alipay "Transport" QR to pay.
3.  **Official Taxi Stands:** At airports (PVG/SHA), **ONLY** use the official taxi queue outside. Ignore everyone inside who says "Taxi?". The official ones use meters and are regulated.

### Problem
Tourists struggle to hail taxis or pay cashless in China.

### Root Cause
- Many taxis prefer mobile apps (Didi) over cash.  
- Apps often require local phone numbers.

### Fix / Solution
- Install Didi app with foreign-friendly registration.  
- Carry small cash as backup.---
