# Diapers & Baby Supplies

**Last Updated:** Jan 2026 | **Author:** TechDadShanghai

### Problem
Finding baby products in foreign cities.

### Root Cause
- Some stores do not stock foreign brands.  
- Confusing store locations.

### Fix / Solution
- Use map apps to locate supermarkets & mother-baby stores.  
- Confirm stock via app / call before going.

### Miles’ Tips
- Major chains: Toys “R” Us, BHG, CitySuper  
- Carry baby wipes & small essentials in backpack.

### Recommendations
- 1-2 reliable stores per district  
- Check online supermarket delivery for emergencies.

### Local Hacks / Money-saving Tips
- Buy small packs at convenience stores for immediate needs.  
- Combine store promotions with mobile coupons.
