## Why Open Source? ğŸ”

ChinaOps is open-source because we believe navigating a country shouldn't be a "black box" operation.

- **Transparency & Verifiability**: Every step in our SOPs is open for inspection. There are no hidden affiliate links, no paid rankings, and no secondary agendas. What you see is exactly what we use.
- **Community-Powered "Hot-patches"**: Travel policies and app interfaces in China change at a rapid-fire pace. Open source allows the community to report outdated info and submit corrections in real-time, ensuring the runbook remains accurate for everyone.
- **Access over Anxiety**: We don't believe in "pay-walling" survival tips or manufacturing anxiety to sell a "secret" guide. Essential informationâ€”how to find a hospital, how to pay for a taxi, or how to check milk safetyâ€”should be free and accessible to every traveler and parent.
