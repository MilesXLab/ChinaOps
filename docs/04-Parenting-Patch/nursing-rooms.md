# Nursing & Changing Rooms

**Last Updated:** Jan 2026 | **Author:** TechDadShanghai

### Problem
Parents cannot find clean, safe spaces for infants.

### Root Cause
- Public restrooms often lack baby facilities.  

### Fix / Solution
- Locate nursing rooms in malls, airports, or major tourist spots.  
- Bring portable changing mat.

### Milesâ€™ Tips
- Major malls usually have international standard facilities.  
- Ask staff for directions; not all are signposted.

### Recommendations
- 1-2 reliable locations per district  
- Carry small hygiene kit.

### Local Hacks / Money-saving Tips
- Avoid paid public restrooms; use nursing room facilities.  
- Reuse wipes / mats for multiple children.
