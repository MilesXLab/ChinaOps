# SIM Card & eSIM Options

**Last Updated:** Jan 2026 | **Author:** TechDadShanghai

### Problem
Travelers cannot receive verification codes or make local calls.

### Root Cause
- Foreign phone numbers often not supported for SMS verification in apps.  

### Fix / Solution
- Purchase local SIM with data plan.  
- eSIMs for short-term travelers can be purchased online.

### Milesâ€™ Tips
- Some metro stations sell temporary SIMs.  
- Airport SIM kiosks are convenient but expensive.

### Recommendations
- Airalo (eSIM), China Unicom Tourist SIM  
- Keep QR code of SIM purchase for top-ups.

### Local Hacks / Money-saving Tips
- Share data among family via hotspot to reduce multiple SIM costs.
