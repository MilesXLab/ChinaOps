**A:** No, not without permission. You may reference ChinaOps with attribution for non-commercial purposes, but cannot republish the full content in a paid product. Contact for licensing.

### Q2: Can my travel company recommend ChinaOps in our materials?
**A:** Yes! You can link to and recommend ChinaOps for free. However, you cannot incorporate the actual content into paid materials without permission.

### Q3: Can I translate ChinaOps into Chinese and publish it as a book?
**A:** Not commercially without permission. Non-commercial translations for community benefit are welcome if you contribute back to the project. Commercial publication requires licensing agreement.

### Q4: Can I use ChinaOps in my paid SaaS travel app?
**A:** No, not without permission. SaaS platforms are inherently commercial. Contact for licensing terms.

### Q5: Can I fork this repo and sell improved versions?
**A:** No. The CC BY-NC license prohibits commercial use of the original or derivatives. However, you can contribute improvements to the main project.

### Q6: What if my company wants to sponsor ChinaOps?
**A:** Sponsorships are welcome! Contact for partnership discussions. This differs from commercial use permission‚Äîsponsorship supports the project without monetizing content.

### Q7: Can I use ChinaOps content in my nonprofit organization?
**A:** Yes! Nonprofits are allowed non-commercial use. Just ensure you provide proper attribution.

### Q8: Can I create course materials using ChinaOps?
