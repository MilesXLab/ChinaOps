## üìã The Runbook

### 1. The Traffic Avoidance Protocol
- **Step 1:** When crossing the street, look for **Electric Scooters (Waimai)**. They are silent and often ride against traffic or on sidewalks.
- **Step 2:** Never assume a Green Light means it's safe to walk without looking. Rights-on-red are legal for cars.
- **Verification:** Establish eye contact with drivers/riders before stepping in front of them.

### 2. The Night-time Movement Logic
- **Step 1:** Walk anywhere in the city at any time (2 AM, 4 AM). Avoid poorly lit construction zones simply to prevent trips/falls.
- **Step 2:** If you feel uneasy, head toward the nearest **24H Convenience Store** (FamilyMart, Lawson, 7-11). These are "safe nodes" with CCTV and staff.
- **Verification:** Check the density of people; "empty" streets in central SH are rare and usually just high-end residential.

### 3. The "Lost in System" Recovery
- **Step 1:** If your phone dies and you are lost, find a **5-star Hotel**. Walk into the lobby.
- **Step 2:** Show the concierge your hotel's name (or your printed card).
- **Verification:** They will either call a taxi or help you charge your phone at a hub.
