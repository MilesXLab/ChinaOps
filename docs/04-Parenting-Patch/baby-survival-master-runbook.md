## 3. Transport & Gear (Stroller Protocols)

### High-Speed Rail (HSR)
- **First Class Hack:** Highly recommended. The legendary legroom allows you to keep a stroller unfolded for the baby to nap comfortably.
- **Second Class:** Aim for the **First Row** or **Last Row** of the carriage for extra floor space.
- **Hot Water Node:** Every carriage end has 100¬∞C boiling water for bottle sterilization or mixing formula.

### Metro & Surface
- **Manual Gate:** Do not squeeze through the bifold gates. Go to the **Manual Lane / Green Channel** at the far end; staff will manually scan your ID and open the side gate for strollers.
- **Didi Car Seats:** Taxis and Didi cars in China **almost never** have car seats. Carry a portable harness or stick to the Metro/HSR for maximum safety.
