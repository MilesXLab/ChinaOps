# Lost Passport / Important Documents

**Last Updated:** Jan 2026 | **Author:** TechDadShanghai

### Problem
Lost or stolen passports while traveling.

### Root Cause
- Travelers often leave documents unsecured.

### Fix / Solution
- Report to local police immediately.  
- Contact your embassy for emergency passport.

### Milesâ€™ Tips
- Keep digital copies on phone & cloud.  
- Have backup ID for hotel check-in.

### Recommendations
- Carry passport wallet or neck pouch.  
- Share emergency contacts with travel partner.

### Local Hacks / Money-saving Tips
- Embassy visits may allow faster processing with small fee.  
- Pre-register with embassy travel notice system.
